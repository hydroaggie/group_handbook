3. Student funding opportunities#

Most students are funded through Research Assistantships. However, students are encouraged to seek out external funding opportunties.

Here is an in-exaustive list:

3.1. Graduate Research#

3.1.1. USU#

  • Presidential Doctoral Research Fellowship by Office of Research @USU. More information on current and past awardees from CEE can be found here. Students will receive additional $10,000 stipend on top of their graduate student assistantship for four years.

  • USU Graduate Funding Opportunities include USUSA Graduate Enhancement Award (GEA) and Graduate Research and Creative Opportunities (GRCO) Grant

  • Climate Adaptation Science one-time payment, need to focus on climate related research (matasem has got one)

3.1.2. State grants#

  • Doyle W. Stephens Scholarship for GSL Research $2000. Must work on GSL related research

  • Air and Waste Management Association Great Basin Chapter Scholarship For students who work in the fields of air quality, waste management, environmental management/law, or sustainability.

3.1.3. National grants#

  • AGU Horton Hydrology Research Grant Highly competitive.

  • GSA Student Research Grant Award many receipents each year. Good to practice proposal writing skills.

  • CUAHSI Pathfinder Fellowships <$5000

  • CUAHSI Hydroinformatics Innovation Fellowship <$5000

  • DOE Office of Science Graduate Student Research (SCGSR) Program Limited to US citizens or LPRs

  • AWRA Scholarships $2000

  • American Water Works Association (AWWA) scholarship $1500

  • NSF Earth Sciences Postdoctoral Fellowships (EAR-PF) Prestigious award

3.2. Undergraduate Research#

  • University undergraduate researcher: https://research.usu.edu/ur/ (Deadline Nov./Apr.)

  • Engineering college undergraduate research program: https://engineering.usu.edu/research/eurp/index

  • Science Undergraduate Laboratory Internships (SULI)

  • AWRA Scholarships $2000

  • American Water Works Association (AWWA) scholarship $1000

  • Doyle W. Stephens Scholarship for GSL Research $2000. Must work on GSL related research

3.3. Travel Grants#

  • USU Travel grant (\(300 from Dept. and \)300 from College)

    • USU Graduate Student Travel Award $300

    • College of Engineering Travel Award $300

  • AGU/GSA conferences offer various travel grants.

    • AGU Student Travel Grants $1000

    • AGU Lloyd V. Berkner Travel Fellowship $2500
